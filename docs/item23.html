Yoghurt Banana Smoothie

What does it consists of?
  • 1 cup plain greek yogurt
  • 30 gm whey protein
  • 6 almonds
  • 1/2 cup soya milk
  • 2 banana
  • ice cubes as required
How To Make

Step 1 Blend whey protein in Greek yoghurt To prepare this recipe, add whey protein powder in a blender along with plain Greek yoghurt and blend until smooth.

Step 2 Blend bananas with soy milk in the Greek yoghurt mixture Next, peel and add bananas in the blender with a bit of soy milk and blend once again. Put in the crushed ice and stir for some time. Leave it in the fridge for some time until it's cold.

Step 3 Garnish with almonds & serve chilled Once chilled, pour in a bowl and garnish with almonds for that healthy serving.
