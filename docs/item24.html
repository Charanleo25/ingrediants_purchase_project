Protein French Toast

What does it consists of?
  • 4 egg whites
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 8 slices whole wheat bread
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup
  • 4 tablespoon vanilla protein powder
How To Make

Step 1 To begin with, take a deep mixing bowl and add peanut butter (melted) followed by vanilla extract, maple syrup, salt, almond milk, egg whites and vanilla protein powder. Mix well using a wooden spoon until you get a running consistency.

Step 2 Now, lay the whole wheat bread slices ( high protein) on a clean surface (or tray) and pour the prepared maple mixture on it. Allow the bread to soak the mixture completely for a minute or two.

Step 3 Meanwhile, put a non-stick pan on medium flame and transfer the bread slices on it. Cook until you get a golden brown shade and a crispy texture. Serve hot and fresh!
