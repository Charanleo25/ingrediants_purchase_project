Protein Bun Bread

If you are looking for a healthy bread recipe, then this Protein Bun Bread recipe is perfect for you! Made with whey, oat flour, egg whites, vanilla protein powder and coconut flour, this easy-to-make recipe can make for a lip-smacking breakfast, when served with butter or honey. You will be amazed to know that this bread recipe is high in protein content and will be great for someone who is into body building. The versatility of this delightful bun recipe allows it to be paired with anything from a soup to a salad. Try this simple recipe and relish it with your loved ones on occasions like picnic, game night and road trip.

What does it consists of?
  • egg whites
  • 1/2 cup vanilla protein powder
  • 1/2 cup oat flour
  • 2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoon flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
How To Make

Step 1 Mix the oats flour and protein powder Preheat your oven to 160 degree Celsius. Take a mixing bowl and add the oats flour followed by the protein powder ( flavored or unflavored), egg whites, flour (coconut flour), baking powder and salt. Mix the ingredients well.

Step 2 Bake the buns Now, carefully divided the prepared mixture in small buns of equally shape. Then place the prepared buns in a baking tray. Make sure to leave space between them so that the bun can expand and rise while it gets baked.

Step 3 Your protein buns are ready to relish! Now, bake the buns for 20 minutes or until they expand in size and have a crispy crust. Prick a fork or knife carefully in the bun and if it comes out clean, then your bun is prepared. Allow the buns to cool down before you serve them.
