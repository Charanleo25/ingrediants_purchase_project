Homemade Protein Powder

Tired of consuming the same boring Whey powders to meet the daily protein needs? Then try this simple yet delicious desi style protein powder made at the comfort of home. Packed with the goodness of nuts, seeds, aromatic spices, this super healthy protein powder is loaded with nutrients and fiber, which is not only great for boosting muscle strength, but at the same time works great for overall immunity. The fiber in this protein powder works as a natural laxative, which supports gut health, boosts metabolic rate that helps in better weight management. So, try this simple protein powder at home and mix it with milk and reap the benefits.

What does it consists of?
  • 1/2 cup almonds
  • 3 tablespoon sunflower seeds
  • 3 tablespoon raisins
  • 1/2 cup pistachios
  • 3 tablespoon sesame seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon green cardamom
  • 3 tablespoon melon seeds
  • 1/2 cup cashews
  • 1 teaspoon fennel seeds
How To Make

Step 1 Roast the ghee To make this simple protein powder, take a pan and add ghee to the pan. Once the ghee turns warm, add in the almonds, peanuts, and pistachios. Toss and transfer to a plate.

Step 2 Chop and mix Next, deseed the dates and chop the anjeer. Take a blender and add in the roasted almonds, peanuts, pistachios, dates, anjeer, seeds. Grind them nicely.
