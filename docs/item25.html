Super Seeds Chocolate Smoothie

What does it consists of?
  • 1 banana
  • 2 tablespoon flax seeds
  • 1 tablespoon milk chocolate
  • 2 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 1 tablespoon cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 teaspoon coffee beans
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
How To Make

Step 1 To prepare this smoothie recipe, firstly, add the chia seeds to a grinder and grind it to get fine powder. Transfer this in to blender jar and add almond milk, banana, flax seeds, protein powder, cocoa powder, and vanilla extract, and blend it on high speed until all the ingredients have dissolved.

Step 2 Now, add coffee beans along with sugar and blend again. Once you get the desired consistency, pour it in a glass and garnish with grated chocolate. Serve it immediately!
